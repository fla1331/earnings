Pro Tips for Designing a Bestselling Ebook Cover

Even with a powerful tool like Ebook Cover Creator, a few design principles can make your cover truly shine:

  1. Keep it Simple: Cluttered covers confuse readers. Focus on one main image or strong visual concept.
  2. Readability is Key: Ensure your title and author name are legible even at thumbnail size. Choose clear, easy-to-read fonts.
  3. Convey Genre: Use colors, imagery, and typography that instantly signal your book's genre. A fantasy novel should look different from a business book.
  4. Use High-Quality Imagery: Pixelated or low-resolution images scream unprofessional. Always use sharp, high-res graphics.
  5. Check for Contrast: Ensure good contrast between text and background to enhance readability.
  6. Get Feedback: Before finalizing, ask trusted friends or your target audience for their opinion. A fresh pair of eyes can spot issues you missed.
  7. Study Your Competition: Look at the bestselling books in your genre. What are their covers doing well? Adapt, but don't copy.
